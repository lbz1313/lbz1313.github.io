Batok Tools

The Batok tool is a traditional Filipino hand tool that is made up of natural materials such as citrus thorns, bamboo sticks, or bone. The mambabatok (tattoo artist) uses the tool in a hand-tapping motion. This cultural practice of ancestral tattooing is a sacred part of Filipino culture. This ritual of tattooing can often involve prayers and traditional motifs that hold cultural significance representing lineage, courage, and identity. This method of tattooing is still practiced and celebrated to this day.
