Get Involved

Memberships

Become a member of the Sacred Ink Museum and support the preservation of tattoo history. Members receive free admission, early access to exhibitions, and exclusive updates on new collections and events.

Ways to Give

Your donations help us preserve tattoo history, support artists, and expand access to our collections. Contributions directly fund exhibitions, archival work, and educational programming.

Volunteer

Join our community by volunteering at the Sacred Ink Museum. Volunteers assist with events, exhibitions, and community outreach while gaining hands-on experience in museum work.
